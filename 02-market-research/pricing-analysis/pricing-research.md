# Pricing Research: Lightweight Calendar Apps Market

**Last Updated:** January 2026

---

## Executive Summary

The lightweight calendar app market shows diverse pricing strategies ranging from **free/freemium** models to **$29/month** premium AI-powered solutions. Key findings:

- **Freemium dominates**: 8 of 10 apps offer free tiers to drive user acquisition
- **Price range**: Paid tiers span $8-$29/user/month for individual plans
- **Lifetime deals**: TidyCal disrupts with $29-79 one-time payment model
- **AI premium**: Apps with AI features (Motion, Reclaim.ai) command higher prices ($18-29/month)
- **Team pricing**: Typically $15-20/user/month with volume discounts at 30+ seats

## Pricing Model Analysis

### Dominant Pricing Strategies

| Strategy | Companies | Characteristics |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Freemium** | Calendly, Cal.com, TidyCal, Notion, Reclaim, SavvyCal | Free tier drives adoption; conversion to paid |
| **Subscription (per-seat)** | Calendly, Cal.com, Reclaim, SavvyCal, Motion | Scales with team size |

## Key Insights for Product Strategy

### 1. Freemium is Essential
- 80% of competitors offer meaningful free tiers
- Free users serve as marketing and viral growth drivers
- Conversion typically happens when users need team features or integrations

### 2. Sweet Spot Pricing: $10-15/user/month
- Most common paid entry point across the market
- Balances accessibility with revenue sustainability
- Volume discounts typically start at 30+ seats

### 3. AI Commands Premium
- Motion and Reclaim.ai charge 2-3x more than basic schedulers
- AI features justify $18-29/month pricing
- Growing expectation but not yet table stakes

### 4. Lifetime Deals Create Disruption
- TidyCal's $29-79 model pressures subscription competitors
- Appeals strongly to freelancers and cost-conscious users
- Sustainable only with low operational overhead

### 5. Enterprise Starts at $15,000+/year
- SSO, SCIM, and compliance features unlock enterprise pricing
- Custom pricing allows value-based negotiation
- Enterprise segment represents ~50% of total market value

## Recommendations for New Entrant

Based on market analysis and your product vision targeting freelancers/solopreneurs:

### Suggested Pricing Structure

| Tier | Price | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Free** | $0 | Individual users, basic scheduling |
| **Pro** | $8-10/mo | Freelancers, advanced integrations |
| **Team** | $12-15/user/mo | Small teams, collaboration features |
| **Business** | $20-25/user/mo | Growing businesses, analytics, priority support |

### Differentiation Opportunities

1. **Undercut Calendly** on team pricing ($12 vs $16)
2. **Match TidyCal** with optional lifetime deal for early adopters
3. **Free tier** must include enough value to drive word-of-mouth
4. **AI features** as premium add-on, not core requirement initially
