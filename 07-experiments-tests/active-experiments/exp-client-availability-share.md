# Experiment: Shareable Client Availability View

**Status:** Active
**Date:** 2026-01-13

---

## Goal

**We believe** offering a shareable "busy/free" calendar view for clients **for** freelancers with 3+ active clients **will result in** reduced status update emails and higher retention.

**Success metric:** 30-day retention from 68% to 75%

---

## Design

**Type:** Beta
**Variants:** Control (no feature) vs Treatment (client availability sharing enabled)
**Duration:** 4 weeks (ends 2026-02-10)
