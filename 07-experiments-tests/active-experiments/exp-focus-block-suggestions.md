**Status:** Active
**Date:** 2026-01-06

## Goal

**We believe** showing AI-suggested focus time blocks based on user patterns **for** users with 5+ meetings/week **will result in** increased weekly active usage and higher retention.

**Success metric:** WAU from 38% to 45%

---

## Design

**Type:** A/B Test
**Variants:** Control (manual focus blocks only) vs Treatment (AI suggestions + manual)
**Duration:** 4 weeks (ends 2026-02-03)
