# Experiment: Upgrade Prompt After Booking Success

**Status:** Active
**Date:** 2026-01-10

---

## Goal

**We believe** showing an upgrade prompt after 10 successful bookings **for** free tier users **will result in** higher conversion rates than limit-based prompts.

**Success metric:** Prompt-to-upgrade conversion from 4% to 8%

---

## Design

**Type:** A/B Test
**Variants:** Control (prompt at booking link limit) vs Treatment (prompt after 10 bookings)
**Duration:** 3 weeks (ends 2026-01-31)
