# Experiment: Signup Flow Sync Prompt Placement

**Status:** Completed
**Date:** 2025-11-15

---

## Goal

**We believe** moving the calendar sync prompt to the first step of onboarding **for** new freelancer signups **will result in** higher activation rates (calendar sync within 7 days).

**Success metric:** 7-day activation rate from 32% to 45%

---

## Design

**Type:** A/B Test
**Variants:** Control (sync prompt on step 3) vs Treatment (sync prompt on step 1)
**Duration:** 4 weeks

---

## Outcome

**Result:** Ship

**What we learned:**
- Treatment achieved 48% activation rate vs 31% control (+17pp lift)
- Users who sync immediately are 3x more likely to return within 7 days
- No negative impact on signup completion rate

**Next steps:**
- Ship sync-first flow to all users
- Explore adding Google vs Outlook choice on landing page
